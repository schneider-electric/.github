<h2>Welcome to the Schneider Electric Open Source Program Office (OSPO)</h2>

We're excited about open source and its positive impact on our company and the wider tech community. This page outlines our mission, activities, and how you can get involved.

**Our Mission**

- To encourage and support the use of open source software within Schneider Electric.
- To promote and facilitate open source contributions from our employees.
- To ensure we are compliant with open source licenses.
- To foster a culture of collaboration and knowledge sharing around open source.

**What We Do**

- Provide guidance and resources on using, contributing to, and releasing open source software.
- Offer training and workshops on open source best practices.
- Maintain an inventory of open source projects used and created by Schneider Electric.
- Develop and enforce open source policies and procedures.
- Represent Schneider Electric in the open source community.

**Get Involved**

- Visit the Schneider Electric Manifesto: [Manifesto](https://www.se.com/ww/en/about-us/open-source/manifesto.jsp)
- Contribute to our projects: [GitHub organization](https://github.com/schneider-electric)

We believe in the power of open source to drive innovation and collaboration. Join us on our journey!

The Schneider Electric OSPO team
